Cast

Mark Hamill

Mark Hamill is best known for his portrayal of Luke Skywalker in the original Star Wars trilogy - Hvězdné války (1977), Star Wars: Epizoda V - Impérium vrací úder (1980), and Star Wars: Epizoda VI - Návrat Jediho (1983) - a role he reprised in Star Wars: Sila se probouzi (2015) and Star Wars: Poslední z Jediu (2017). He also starred and co-starred in the films Corvette Summer (1978), Velká červená jednička (1980), and Kingsman: The Secret Service (2014). Hamill's extensive voice acting work includes a long-standing role as the Joker, commencing with Batman (1992).
